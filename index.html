why choose me?

Divya - A self-taught young entrepreneur and that’s how I roll proudly.

CraftWithDivya Started leisurely Since 2019 with a motive to ‘Make Memories’ to develop Specialised handmade gifts but little did I know it took me half a year to learn about the basics of this business like raw materials and design elements.

One thing that kept me going was ‘Practice makes it perfect

To suit the proverb I spent almost 2 years developing a product to my expectation and perfection starting from sourcing raw materials, creating a product, calculating cost, packing and shipping and most importantly customer feedback. These traits have kept me going and grinding to give you the best.

As a brand #CraftWithDivya is all about making memories with unique ideas and products that are customisable to your needs and that’s how we specialise apart from others.

Looking back at my 3 years journey and all the happy moments that I have made possible and now with my product lineup and sales increasing day by day we are now live here as a one stop website and also active on Instagram as always. Nothing but Thank you can’t suffice your support. Keep supporting your next door entrepreneur.
